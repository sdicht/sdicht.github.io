# Syllabus

## Course Description
This course explores literary modernism through key texts, critical essays, and historical context.

## Learning Outcomes
By the end of the semester, students will:
1. Analyze major modernist texts.
2. Relate literature to its historical and cultural contexts.
3. Produce original critical writing.

## Assessment
- Participation: 10%
- Short Paper: 20%
- Midterm Essay: 30%
- Final Project: 40%
